How to Use AlarmTalk

  1. Download and Install: Download the app from GitHub and install the APK on your Android device.
  2. Grant Permissions: Allow necessary permissions including microphone access for voice recording, notification access, and overlay permissions for lock screen display.
  3. Create an Alarm: Tap the "+" button to create a new alarm and set your desired time using the time picker.
  4. Customize: Add a title and note (up to 200 characters).
  5. Enable TTS: Turn on the Text-to-Speech toggle to convert your note to speech.
  6. Set Repeat Days: Choose which days you want the alarm to repeat (everyday, weekdays, weekends, or custom days).
  7. Adjust Volume: Set your preferred alarm volume using the volume slider.
  8. Select Ringtone: Choose your preferred ringtone from the available options with preview functionality.
  9. Configure Snooze: Select your preferred snooze duration from 5-30 minutes.
  10. Set Vibration: Enable or disable vibration for your alarm.
  11. Advanced Settings: Access advanced options to record voice messages and manage recordings.
  12. Save and Activate: Save your alarm and toggle it on to activate.
  13. Wake Up: When your alarm triggers, use the snooze or dismiss buttons to manage it.
